About Me

My Journey in Debate and Public Speaking

Hello! I'm Arissa Nurjannah

I am a passionate debater and public speaker with a focus on both English and Malay debate. Over the years, I have participated in a few debate competitions and understanding of the art of persuasive speaking. My journey in debate began when I was 17, and since then, I have been involved in various debate events.

As the Media & Publicist of the Youth Orator Club, my goal is to create a platform where young individuals can improve their debate and public speaking skills. I believe that debate is not just about arguing; it's about understanding different perspectives, building confidence, and communicating effectively.

I am dedicated to helping others find their love in debating, whether through joining yoc, providing resources, or organizing events. Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to connecting with you!

If you have any question, don't be shy and hit me up on instagram at @xoarissa_
