Introduction to Debating

Debating is not just about arguing; it's about presenting your ideas in a structured, persuasive way. Whether you're debating in English or Malay, these principles will help you excel in any format:

  • Research: Understand your topic thoroughly before you speak.
  • Structure: Organize your speech into clear points that build your argument.
  • Persuasion: Focus on convincing your audience with facts, logic, and emotional appeal.
  • Public Speaking: Engage your audience through voice modulation, body language, and eye contact.
  • Rebuttals: Practice responding effectively to opposing arguments.
